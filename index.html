The Game

You enrolled into an interdimensional school that is connected with other dimensions and universes. In this school, your *extraordinary* classmates come from many places (ex. space). You just try your best to survive throughout your school years and, who knows, you might steal someone's heart along the way :3.



The Club

The Visual Novel Development Club, or VND for short, is a club for making, well, visual novels! We welcome all those who are interested in making games, itch to design characters for a story, or are passionate on telling stories themselves! We'll be making our own visual novel for us to publish! Making everything from sprite illustrations, storyboards for each decision, and even making our own soundtracks!



Note:

Everything is still a work in progress! The more members, the better! We are in need of more programmers and music artists. (⁠╥⁠﹏⁠╥⁠)
