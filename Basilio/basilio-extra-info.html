Prepping for School

He snoozes the alarm and goes back to sleep; he’s always late to school.



Eating Habits

Blood bags and organs of lambs or pigs (e.g. heart, liver, spleen, kidneys, etc.) in a cute lunch box. He watches bento making videos for funs and he does them as well.



Pets (Whether Existing or Potential)

A Bombay or Toyger cat– or just any cat they would pick up from the street.



Allergies

Ash, garlic, salt, vinegar, raw rice, or any other spices. They also don’t like the smell of burning rubber.



Expressing Romance

Quality time. When he does fall in love or have a crush on someone, he would invite them to eat lunch with him, listen to him play in his clubroom, or break school rules. He doesn’t talk a lot during these sessions, but he enjoys their presence in silence.



Meeting Up With Someone

He would still go to the meeting spot, but he peeks around the corner first to get a glimpse of the suitor’s face. If it’s someone he doesn’t recognize, he leaves immediately. If it’s an acquaintance, he listens to what they have to say; if they start the lines of a love confession, he turns them down and then leaves.



Unfortunate Events Involving Murder...
