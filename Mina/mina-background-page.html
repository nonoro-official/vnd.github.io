Mina's Background


Mina is the alchemy professor’s helper. She was produced through means of transmutation from the organic components of a bull, hence her minotaur features, despite her not being related to any at all. In fact, she isn’t related to any creature in general, as her entire being is purely artificial. Not too many people seem to interact with her, so she tends to the school garden instead to pass the time.
