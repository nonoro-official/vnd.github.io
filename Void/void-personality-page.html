Speechless (Literally)

Doesn’t speak much or at all actually, tends to use sign language (commonly uses ASL)



The Observer

Void is quiet and tends to observe a lot, happy to just watch! If you want to speak to the Void, then you’re the one who has to initiate a conversation but even then, they may just stare back at you.


Because of this behavior, most people tend to find the Void quite creepy and so they actually don’t have many friends—a lot of the time, people just tolerate the Void. They’re pretty used to them at this point but still... they just.. stare... and don’t speak... but if u do decide to include the Void in things they are so so very happy!!
